The intent of this project is to compile information
about (almost) ALL the mathematicians of the world.
It is our goal to list all individuals who
have received a doctorate in mathematics.

For each individual we plan to show the following:
  • The complete name of the degree recipient
  • The name of the university which awarded the degree
  • The year in which the degree was awarded
  • The complete name(s) of the advisor(s)
  • The complete name(s) of the student(s)
